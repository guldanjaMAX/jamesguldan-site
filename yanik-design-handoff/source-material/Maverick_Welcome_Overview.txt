Maverick is a global collective of visionary entrepreneurs
who believe business can be the greatest lever for good.

For 18 years, we have gathered founders who connect their
head (business sense), heart (impact), and highest purpose.

We are not a mastermind. We are not a networking group.
We are not a peer advisory board.
