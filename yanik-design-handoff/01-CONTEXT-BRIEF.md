**1. Who is Yanik Silver**

Yanik Silver is a serial entrepreneur, author, and founder of
Maverick1000, a global invite only network of visionary entrepreneurs.
He is most famous in the infopreneur and mastermind worlds for hosting
surreal experiences (zero gravity flights, Necker Island retreats,
jungle ceremonies) and for being the person who will show up at a
business summit in a cosmic sloth creature suit and make it land.

He has exited multiple eight figure businesses. He has written multiple
books including Maverick Startup, Evolved Enterprise, and The Cosmic
Journal. But this site is not about those wins. This site is about what
came after the wins. The internal myth he built to keep playing.

**The mythology he wears**

His personal cosmology has three characters and one goal.

-   **Cebu.** the cosmic sloth. Yanik\'s self avatar. A slow, attentive,
    playful being who climbs the tree of the world and wakes up sleeping
    suns. Tattooed on his body. Drawn in his journals. Worn as a
    creature suit.

-   **The Thousand Suns.** every person he wakes up. Each bright soul
    lit by a previous sun. The goal is a field of 1000 fully awake
    entrepreneurs living their own most alive version.

-   **The Field.** the ecosystem itself. Self activating. Seeds become
    blooms. Blooms become suns. Suns wake up more seeds. It runs on
    warmth, not hustle.

He is the founder of Maverick1000 (the network), publisher of The Cosmic
Journal (his creative practice book), and designer of The Galactic Game
(a life as infinite game framework). All three artifacts are extensions
of the same mythology.

**Who the site is for**

Entrepreneurs who already have the win and are looking for the meaning.
Post exit founders. Eight and nine figure operators who got the thing
and now ask, what now. The site is not a lead magnet. It is a filter. It
is a door that only opens for people who already feel the pull.

**2. Brand voice and copy rules**

**The voice in one line**

> *Mythic, warm, playful, never slick. Toddler readable but adult coded.
> A hand bound picture book a serious founder would take seriously.*

**Do**

-   Write in short sentences. Let white space carry meaning.

-   Use Yanik\'s own words when possible (see vocabulary below).

-   Stay in second person for the reader. First person for Yanik.

-   Treat the reader like they already know. Never explain the myth.
    Show it.

-   Let typography (italic Fraunces serif, Caveat script) carry
    emotional register.

**Do not**

-   Never use emojis.

-   Never use dashes (em dash, en dash, hyphen as sentence pause). Use
    commas, periods, or line breaks instead. This is a hard rule.

-   Never use corporate SaaS language. No \'empower\', no \'unlock\', no
    \'drive results\'.

-   Never use stock CTAs. No \'Learn more\', \'Get started\', \'Sign up
    today\'.

-   Never compete visually with the storytelling. Decoration supports,
    never dominates.

**Yanik\'s vocabulary (use these words)**

-   sun, seed, bloom, field, ray, mirror, tend, wake up, light

-   maverick, sun one, cosmic, infinite game, the field

-   Cebu, the sloth, the cosmic sloth

-   blessing, journey, myth, doorway, ticket

-   journal, doodle, studio, board, archetype

**Archetype CTAs that work**

-   step inside the field

-   turn the page

-   meet the thousand suns

-   take the ticket

-   keep reading

**3. Visual system**

**Color palette**

-   **Paper cream \#f6eeda.** primary background for storybook sections.
    Warm, aged, handmade paper.

-   **Ink brown \#2b2118.** primary text color. Reads like dark pencil
    on paper.

-   **Soft ink \#5a4a38.** secondary text, metadata, whispers.

-   **Burnt orange \#b8471f.** Cebu accent, primary emphasis for
    \'seed\' phase and chapter 1 of myth.

-   **Bloom rose \#c8557f.** \'bloom\' phase color. Growth, aliveness,
    color coming in.

-   **Sun gold \#d89a2a (also \#f2c940 for hero).** \'sun\' phase color.
    Full activation. Also used for hero accents.

-   **Earth green \#6b8e4e.** new \'seed\' phase color (from polish
    brief), indicates potential.

-   **Deep plum \#1a0d2e and \#2f1a4a.** hero night sky background. The
    only dark background on the site.

**Typography stack**

-   **Fraunces (serif).** headline font, italic 300 weight for story
    beats. Warm, literary, editorial.

-   **Inter (sans).** body UI, metadata, buttons. Clean, neutral, does
    not compete with Fraunces.

-   **Caveat (script).** handwritten marginalia, captions, labels on
    torn tape. Yanik\'s voice.

-   **Space Grotesk (mono).** small all caps labels (section IDs,
    eyebrow text). Technical, grounding.

**Core visual patterns**

-   **Storybook layout.** paper background, centered text column max
    720px, large italic Fraunces paragraphs with 36px margins, optional
    Caveat whisper lines beneath.

-   **Ambient doodles.** Yanik\'s actual journal pages positioned behind
    text at 12 percent opacity with multiply blend mode. Texture not
    collage.

-   **Polaroid or pinned art.** when doodles go from ambient to
    featured, they sit on the page with a soft drop shadow as if pinned
    to a wall.

-   **Sun motif (SVG).** hand drawn sun with uneven rays. Lives in the
    manifesto section as a field behind the vow.

-   **Paper ticket.** for the invitation section. Torn perforation,
    stub, serial number.

-   **Cork pinboard.** for the doodle gallery. Masking tape captions in
    handwriting, overlap and tilt.

-   **Cosmic board.** for the Galactic Game. 7x3 grid, archetype
    symbols, YOU ARE HERE lit tile.

**Animation principles**

-   Slow. Always slow. Yanik is Cebu, the slow one. No snap transitions.

-   Fade and drift, never slide. Elements should feel like they surface
    from the paper, not like they swipe in.

-   Scroll triggered reveals at 0.3s ease. Stagger siblings by 80ms.

-   Hero mandala rotates 360 degrees over 80 seconds. That is the tempo
    reference.

**4. The myth in one paragraph**

Long ago the sky was dark and the world was asleep. High in the biggest
tree lived a slow little sloth named Cebu. One day he whispered, the
world needs light. He climbed for a thousand days and found a tree full
of cacao pods. Inside each pod, a sleeping sun. He opened them one by
one until a thousand suns shone back, and the cacao fell to earth as
chocolate for the people. Cebu smiled. His job was never to be the sun.
His job was to wake the suns up. Yanik Silver is the cosmic sloth Cebu.
Same heart. Same job. He walks the field slowly, holding up a mirror to
every sleeping seed so it can see the sun inside itself.

**Field progression (section 02)**

Every person starts as a seed (green, potential, unaware). A warm ray
wakes them up. They become a bloom (rose, brave, wobbly, learning). The
bloom opens all the way and becomes a sun (gold, fully awake, now
lighting others). Every ray of every sun falls on another seed. Seeds
become blooms. Blooms become suns. Suns wake up more seeds. The field
self activates forever.

**7. Technical constraints**

**Deployment**

-   Two commit GitHub deploy pattern via Cloudflare Worker at
    github-deploy.james-d13.workers.dev/deploy.

-   Commit 1 pushes HTML. Commit 2 pushes binary assets base64 encoded.

-   Live URL cache busts with a query param. Verify with curl after
    deploy.

**HTML constraints**

-   Single file index.html. All CSS inline in style blocks per section.
    No external CSS.

-   External fonts from Google Fonts (Fraunces, Inter, Caveat, Space
    Grotesk).

-   All images live in assets/ relative to the HTML.

-   Mobile breakpoint at 860px and 820px (depends on section). Test
    both.

**8. How to work on this project**

**When asked to design a new section**

-   Read the corresponding section card in part 5 above.

-   Check what doodles map to the section (part 6 asset inventory).

-   Match the voice (part 2) and visual system (part 3) before
    generating.

-   Deliver as a standalone HTML preview file first, not edits to
    index.html, until James approves.

-   Include per change confidence scores.

**When asked to polish an existing section**

-   Read the entire section\'s existing markup in index.html first. Do
    not partial read.

-   Make minimal, targeted changes. Keep the section\'s voice intact.

-   Never remove confidence rings, section masts, or the A plus pick
    label structure without explicit permission.

**Red flags**

-   If the request conflicts with the mythology (for example, turning
    Cebu into a Native American tribal figure, which he is not), stop
    and verify.

-   If the request adds corporate SaaS patterns (testimonial carousels,
    feature grids, pricing tables), stop and verify.

-   If the request uses dashes in copy, they must be rewritten before
    shipping.

-   If a new asset is needed, flag it. Do not generate new illustrations
    to match Yanik\'s hand. His hand is his hand.

**Default response format**

-   Ship the file, provide a computer:// link, state what changed in one
    short paragraph, give a confidence score.

-   Do not write long recap summaries of work done. James can read the
    diff.
