What This Looks Like in Practice

One company was sitting on a customer list of 12,000 people but only had two offers: a $2K program and a $25K mastermind. Nothing in between. No way to test if a mid tier offer would work without spending months and thousands on development and ads.

We used AI to analyze their customer data and identified a segment of 1,800 people who had bought the $2K program, engaged heavily, but never upgraded. We built a $7,500 offer in a week, put up a page using AI, and tested it against that segment. 23 buyers in the first 30 days. That offer is now a permanent part of their stack.

Then we did it again the next month with a different segment. One offer per month. Tested with real buyers. Keep what works, kill what doesn't.

Questions

Before you fill out the form

How fast can you actually test an offer?
We typically go from ideation to live test within 3 to 4 weeks. That includes building the offer page, identifying the right segment of your list, and running it against real buyers. What used to take months now happens monthly.
What kind of companies does this work for?
Any company selling mid or high ticket products or services. Consulting, coaching, events, masterminds, SaaS with enterprise tiers, professional services. If your offer is over $1,000 and you have an existing customer or prospect list, this process works.
What if the offer doesn't work?
That's the point. We test small so you fail cheap. A failed offer test with a small sample costs almost nothing compared to a full launch that flops. We learn from it and move to the next one. Most companies test 3 to 4 offers before they find a winner that sticks.
Do I need a big customer list for this to work?
You need enough data to segment. A list of 1,000+ customers or prospects is usually enough to start. The AI does the heavy lifting of finding the right segment within that list, so quality matters more than size.
What makes this different from hiring an agency?
An agency assigns you to their B team. I'm the A team and the only team. You get 15 years of offer strategy and psychology expertise paired with custom AI tools, not a junior strategist running your account between 46 others.
