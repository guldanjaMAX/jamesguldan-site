5. Email and SMS Communications

When you purchase a product or opt in to our list, you consent to receive email messages from us. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link.

SMS Program. When you provide your phone number and check the SMS consent box at checkout, you consent to receive text messages from James Guldan related to your purchase. Message types include order confirmation, product access instructions, and onboarding guidance. Message frequency varies and is based on your purchase activity. You will typically receive no more than 3 messages per transaction. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS at any time, reply STOP to any message. After opting out you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages. For help, reply HELP or contact james@jamesguldan.com. SMS consent is not a condition of purchase — providing your phone number and opting in is always optional.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All categories of personal data listed in this policy exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the text message services needed to deliver messages to you. We will not share your opt-in to an SMS program with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with the services of that program.

We may share your information, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third-party service providers who assist us in delivering text messages (such as our SMS platform and carrier aggregators). These vendors are bound by confidentiality obligations and are prohibited from using your data for their own marketing purposes.

Phone numbers and SMS consent records are stored securely and retained as required by applicable law and carrier regulations.
