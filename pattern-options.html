01
You've Outgrown Your Brand
You built something that worked. Then you grew. Now your website, your offers, and your positioning reflect the person you were two years ago. Not who you are now.
02
You're Stuck Between Identities
The old you built the business. The new you knows it needs to change. But you can't see what comes next because you're standing too close to it.
03
Nobody Will Tell You The Truth
Your team agrees with you. Your friends encourage you. Your coach validates you. Nobody sits across from you and says "that's the thing that's broken."
