What This Is

You don't have a strategy problem. You have a mirror problem.

Every coach and consultant I've ever talked to knows exactly what's broken. They can feel it in their chest every time a prospect goes quiet. Every time a sales call doesn't close. Every time they refresh their inbox hoping today is different.

The problem isn't strategy. The problem is that nobody in your life will sit with you for two uninterrupted hours and ask you the right questions without an agenda. Without trying to sell you something. Without filtering you through their own framework.

I built a two-hour AI interview that does what no coach, no mastermind, and no course can do. It pulls your story apart at the seams and gives you an empirical, researched answer about exactly what niche you could dominate — named competitors, pricing data, a credibility score from 1 to 10, and the specific market opportunity your background sets you up for.
